What are outbound call center services?

Outbound call services are a type of service provided by call centers where they make outbound calls to customers on behalf of their clients. The call can be of various types, such as telemarketing, sales, fund-raising, and surveys.

Some of the various outbound call center services include:

  • Lead generation services
  • Database management
  • Customer acquisition services
  • Cold calling campaigns
  • Direct sales services
What is B2B outbound calling?

B2B outbound calling can be defined as an activity where a company reaches out to potential business customers through voice calls to communicate the value of its product/service and in the process establish a long-term relationship. B2B outbound calling requires a special set of skills different from telemarketing since the emphasis lies in building relationships for long-term growth rather than purely selling products.

How to handle outbound calls?

Here are a few tips to effectively handle outbound calls:

  • Pre-call Preparation is a Must: One of the important steps while making an outbound call. By analyzing the prospect’s website, following him/her on social media platforms keeping a track of their online activity gives you a fair insight into their personality opening avenues of conversation.
  • Radiate confidence: It’s important that you sound confident while conversing with your prospect. Giving them the impression that you aren’t too confident in your approach can backfire and lead to rejections.
  • Follow a direct approach: Befriending your prospects for small talk on current happenings can help strengthen your bond with them. However, at times, depending on the situation it’s important to be direct and state the real motive behind the call.
  • Listening is equally important: Outbound calls are not only about speaking but also about listening. You must pay attention to what the prospect is sharing, allow yourself to understand their business, and then wait to ask a relevant follow-up question.
  • Track and Measure Consistently: Keeping a track of your prospects is crucial. Most outbound calling services rely on spreadsheets for this process, while some prefer CRMs. Keeping a track of leads on pen and paper is outdated. With multiple digital tools at your service, you can effortlessly communicate and share the lead’s status with your team.
