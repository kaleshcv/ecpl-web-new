Our Debt Collection Process

  • Account is placed for debt collection
  • The client forwards every minute detail regarding the debt along with other supporting documentation
  • The claim is thoroughly scrutinized, and accurate data is entered into our debt collection system
  • Demand letter is generated and sent to debtor via email, mail or fax. An acknowledgment letter regarding the same is sent to the client and phone contact begins
  • Our debt collection team use their experience and expertise to resolve disputes, recover payment and/or get accurate commitment on debt resolution
  • In case we receive any payment commitment from the debtor, we follow-up on them and ensure that the payment is recovered
  • If case debtor fails to comply with the commitment or doesn’t cooperate, we make final pre-legal collection attempts
  • We send all the details and recommendations to the client, including particular for sending the file to an affiliated attorney
  • Once we obtain client’s consent, we send the claim and other details to our affiliated attorney and instruct him/her to examine the claim for 60 days. In case the client decides not to authorize this move, the claim is worked on for additional 60 days and finally the file is closed
  • After completing the investigation, the attorney provides a detailed recommendation of whether or not to initiate a legal action. If they recommend to go ahead with the legal action, they will provide us with their requirements for law suit
  • We will collect the required suit requirements from the client and send it to the attorney, who will then prepare and file the lawsuit. In case the client does not provide suit requirements or is not willing to file the law suit, we will work on the claim for another 60 days and finally close it
  • The complaint is served
  • Attorney files for default judgment if there is no answer from the debtor. If an answer is filed by debtor, the trail process begins, and a date is set
  • The attorney files for a Writ of Attachment if the judgment is awarded in our favour
  • Attorney attempt to locate and verify debtor assets
  • Attorney initiates liens/ garnishment orders/ bank levies/ etc. in line with the judgment
