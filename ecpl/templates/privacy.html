Privacy Policy

We take steps to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of the data that you share with us.

  • In order to best provide you with our products and services, we may share your personal information with:

We Leverage Information to Serve You Better (Cookies)

  • We use cookies to help provide you with better and more personalized service

  • We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices put forth by these sites

GDPR Compliance (For European Union visitors to our website)

  • Basic identification details such as name, telephone number, email address, company name and country name, along with web data comprising mainly of IP address, cookies and system location are stored in secure locations
  • We store these details solely for the purpose of communicating with clients and potential customers about our products and services
